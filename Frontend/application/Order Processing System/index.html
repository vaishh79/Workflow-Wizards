Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process an order on your website?

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

Can I make changes to my order after it's been placed?

Once an order is submitted, changes may not be possible, but please contact our support team for assistance.

How are shipping fees calculated?

Shipping fees are calculated based on the destination and weight of the items in your order.

When will I receive my invoice after placing an order?

Invoices are generated and sent via email immediately after your order is confirmed.

What payment methods are accepted for orders?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other popular online payment methods.

Can I request a copy of my invoice if I lose it?

Yes, you can request a copy of your invoice by contacting our customer support team.
